The movie "Interstellar" is a science fiction drama directed by Christopher Nolan. The film was released in 2014.

The story is set in the future, where Earth is facing environmental problems such as drought and famine. The main character, named Cooper, is a former pilot and now a farmer. He receives a message from an unknown source, which leads him to a secret organization working on saving humanity.

Cooper joins a mission to embark on a space journey in search of a new habitable world for humanity. He leaves his family behind and ventures into deep space with a group of astronauts to explore a wormhole that could provide access to another galaxy.

The film explores themes of time, space, love, human survival, and our place in the universe. It presents complex scientific concepts such as Albert Einstein's theory of general relativity and black holes.
